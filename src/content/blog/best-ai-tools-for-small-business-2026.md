## Common Mistakes Small Businesses Make with AI

**1. Trying too many tools at once.**
Pick one AI assistant and master it. Then add a second tool for a specific workflow. Spreading attention across 10 tools means mastering none.

**2. Not training AI on their brand.**
Generic outputs come from generic prompts. Spend 2 hours creating a "brand brief" document — your tone, target customer, key messages, product/service details. Paste it into every AI conversation. The output quality jumps dramatically.

**3. Expecting AI to replace human judgment.**
AI writes the draft. You make the strategic decisions. The business owners getting the best results use AI for execution, not strategy.

**4. Skipping the review step.**
AI makes mistakes. Always review outputs before publishing or sending to customers. A 5-minute review prevents 99% of problems.

**5. Not measuring time savings.**
Track how long tasks took before and after AI adoption. This data justifies the subscription cost and shows you where to invest next.
