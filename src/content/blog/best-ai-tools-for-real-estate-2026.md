## FAQs

### Do AI tools really help sell more homes?
Yes — the data is overwhelming. Agents using AI tools for lead follow-up, pricing, and marketing consistently outperform those who don't. The most impactful tools are AI CRMs and chatbots that ensure no lead goes unfollowed.

### Will AI replace real estate agents?
No. Real estate is fundamentally a relationship and negotiation business. AI handles the repetitive work (follow-ups, descriptions, staging, data analysis) so agents can focus on what humans do best: building trust and closing deals.

### What's the best first AI tool to try?
Start with your CRM — [Follow Up Boss](AFFILIATE_LINK_FOLLOW_UP_BOSS) is the highest-impact single tool. If your follow-up game is already strong, add [Virtual Staging AI](AFFILIATE_LINK_VIRTUAL_STAGING) for the lowest-cost instant impact.

### Are virtually staged photos legal?
Yes, but most MLSs require disclosure that photos are virtually staged. Always label virtual staging clearly to avoid misleading buyers. Check your local MLS rules for specific requirements.
