## Key Features in 2026

### 1. GPT-4o: The Core Engine

GPT-4o is OpenAI's most capable publicly available model. In 2026, it excels at:

- **Multimodal understanding** — Upload screenshots, PDFs, images, and it analyzes them intelligently

## Performance Deep-Dive

### Writing & Content Creation

**Score: 4/5**

ChatGPT produces solid, readable content. For blog posts, emails, and marketing copy, it delivers well-structured output. However, compared to [Jasper AI](#affiliate-jasper) (which is fine-tuned specifically for marketing) or [Claude](#affiliate-claude) (which writes more naturally), ChatGPT's default writing style can feel slightly generic.

**Where it shines:** Technical documentation, long-form articles, summarization  
**Where it falls short:** Brand voice consistency, poetry, highly creative fiction

**Pro tip:** Use a detailed system prompt specifying your brand voice, target audience, and tone to dramatically improve output quality.

### Coding

**Score: 4.5/5**

ChatGPT is exceptional at coding tasks. In our 2026 tests:

- **Bug fixing accuracy:** 87% on a test suite of 50 common Python/JavaScript bugs
- **Code generation:** Produced working code on first attempt 73% of the time for medium-complexity tasks
- **Explanation quality:** Excellent at explaining what code does and why

For coding, ChatGPT and [Claude](#affiliate-claude) are neck-and-neck, with GitHub Copilot (powered by GPT-4o) winning for IDE-integrated workflow. See our [GitHub Copilot vs ChatGPT comparison](/cashcow-seo-site/blog/github-copilot-vs-chatgpt-2026) for a detailed breakdown.

### Reasoning & Analysis

**Score: 4/5**

ChatGPT's reasoning is strong but occasionally overconfident — it will state incorrect information with the same confidence as correct information. Always verify important facts.

For complex multi-step reasoning, **o1** (OpenAI's reasoning model, available to Plus users) significantly outperforms GPT-4o but is slower and more expensive to run.

**Compared to [Claude](#affiliate-claude):** Claude 3.5 Sonnet edges out GPT-4o on nuanced reasoning tasks and is less likely to confidently state false information.

### Real-Time Information

**Score: 4/5**

Web browsing is enabled by default in ChatGPT Plus. It handles current events, recent product launches, and today's prices well. However, [Perplexity AI](#affiliate-perplexity) remains the gold standard for research with citations — ChatGPT's citations are less consistent and harder to verify.

## What ChatGPT Does Poorly

Be aware of these persistent limitations:

❌ **Hallucination** — Still invents facts, citations, and statistics. Never use ChatGPT output in academic or legal work without verification.

❌ **Math errors** — GPT-4o struggles with complex arithmetic. Always use Code Interpreter for calculations.

❌ **Sycophancy** — ChatGPT tends to agree with you even when you're wrong. Push back and it often reverses its position without new evidence.

❌ **Real-time pricing/availability** — Web browsing is inconsistent; product prices and availability aren't always current.

❌ **Long conversation degradation** — Performance dips in very long conversations. Start fresh sessions for new tasks.
