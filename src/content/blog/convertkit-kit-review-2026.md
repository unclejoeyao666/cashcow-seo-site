## What Is ConvertKit (Kit)?

Kit is an email marketing platform built specifically for content creators — bloggers, podcasters, YouTubers, course creators, and newsletter writers. Unlike generic email tools like Mailchimp, Kit was designed from the ground up for people who sell digital products, memberships, or courses.

**Key differentiators:**
- Subscriber-centric (tag-based, not list-based)
- Built-in digital product sales and paid newsletter features
- Creator Network for cross-promotion
- Visual automations that are actually usable

## Key Features

### Email Broadcasts
Send one-off emails to your list or segments. The editor is clean and optimized for text-heavy creator content — not the cluttered drag-and-drop builders of enterprise tools.

### Visual Automations
Create automated email sequences triggered by tags, purchases, or link clicks. The visual canvas makes complex funnels surprisingly easy to build.

### Creator Network
Discover other Kit creators to cross-promote newsletters. This is genuinely unique — a built-in growth tool that can accelerate subscriber growth without paid ads.

### Commerce & Paid Newsletters
Sell digital products, courses, or paid newsletter subscriptions directly through Kit. No third-party integration required (though integrations with Teachable, Thinkific, and others are available).

### Landing Pages & Forms
Build unlimited landing pages and opt-in forms without needing a separate tool like Leadpages.
