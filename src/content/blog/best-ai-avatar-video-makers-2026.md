## Limitations You Should Know About

No AI avatar tool is perfect. Here's what to watch for:

1. **Uncanny valley risk.** Lower-tier avatars can feel "off." Stick with Synthesia or HeyGen if realism matters for your brand.
2. **Custom avatar costs.** Cloning your own likeness typically requires enterprise plans ($200+/mo) and sometimes a one-time setup fee.
3. **Rendering time.** Most tools take 2–5 minutes per minute of video. Real-time streaming is still in beta on most platforms.
4. **Gesture limitations.** Avatars can talk, nod, and gesture — but they can't interact with physical objects or demonstrate software (pair with screen recording for that).
5. **Ethical concerns.** Always use consent-verified avatars. Never clone someone without permission. Most platforms now enforce strict consent protocols.
6. **Script dependency.** The video is only as good as the script. Bad writing + great avatar = still a bad video.
