## FAQ

### Is AI writing replacing human writers?

No — it's replacing bad human writers and amplifying great ones. The writers who thrive in 2026 are those who use AI as a force multiplier, not a replacement. If you're still writing from scratch for every piece, you're working 5x harder than necessary.

### Will Google penalize AI-written content?

Google doesn't penalize AI content — it penalizes *low-quality* content. AI-generated content that's accurate, helpful, and well-structured ranks just fine. The problem is unedited AI slop. Always add human expertise and editing.

### How much can I realistically produce with AI writing tools?

A skilled writer using Claude Pro can comfortably produce 10–15 high-quality 1,500-word articles per month instead of 2–3. That's a 5x output multiplier at the same quality level.

### Should I disclose AI-assisted content?

Best practice in 2026 is transparency. If AI wrote the first draft and you substantially edited and verified it, disclosure is optional but recommended. If you published raw AI output, disclose it.

### Which AI writing tool has the best free plan?

Claude has a free tier (limited usage). Writesonic and Copy.ai both offer free trials. For ongoing use, $20/month tools are the minimum worth considering for serious content work.
