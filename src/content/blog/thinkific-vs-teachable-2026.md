## Affiliate Programs: The Key Differentiator

If you're evaluating these platforms through an **affiliate lens**, the difference is stark:

### Thinkific Affiliate Program

- **Commission:** 30% recurring

- **Cookie:** **90 days**
- **Platform:** PartnerStack (professional, reliable)
- **Payout:** Monthly, on the 13th

**Example:** You refer someone to Thinkific Start ($74/mo). You earn **$22.20/month forever**. After 12 months: $266. After 36 months: $799.

### Teachable Affiliate Program

- **Commission:** 30% recurring
- **Duration:** **1 year cap** (then commission stops)

- **Payout:** Monthly

**Example:** Same referral at $74/mo equivalent. You earn the same for 12 months — then $0. After 36 months: still $266.

**The verdict:** Thinkific's lifetime cookie and 90-day attribution window make it **significantly more valuable** for affiliates building long-term passive income. After year one, Thinkific compounds; Teachable stops.
