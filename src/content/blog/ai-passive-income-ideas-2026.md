## Frequently Asked Questions

### How much can you realistically make with AI passive income?

Beginners typically see $200–$500/month within 3–6 months if consistent. With 12 months of compounding effort across 2–3 methods, $2,000–$5,000/month is achievable.

### Do I need technical skills?

Not for most of these. Digital products, stock art, and content sites require no coding. Micro-SaaS requires more but no-code tools have made it accessible.

### Which AI tool is most useful for passive income?

[Claude AI](#affiliate-claude) offers the highest-quality output for written content, analysis, and coding assistance. For visual work, [Midjourney](#affiliate-midjourney) remains the gold standard.

### Is AI passive income sustainable?

Yes, if you build on defensible platforms (your own site, diversified marketplaces) and focus on quality over quantity. Pure AI content farms without human value-add are declining.

### How do I avoid the "passive income trap"?

Passive income still requires upfront active work. Budget 3–6 months of consistent effort before expecting meaningful returns. Think of it as investing, not a get-rich-quick scheme.
