## FAQs

### Can AI website builders replace web developers?
For simple to medium-complexity sites, absolutely yes. A small business, portfolio, or blog can be built entirely with AI. For complex web apps, custom integrations, or enterprise sites, you'll still need developers.

### Are AI-built websites good for SEO?
Most modern AI builders generate SEO-friendly code with proper meta tags, mobile responsiveness, and fast loading times. Framer and Wix score particularly well in our Lighthouse tests.

### Can I switch from an AI builder to a custom site later?
Some builders (like 10Web/WordPress) make this easy since you own the WordPress site. Walled-garden builders (Wix, Jimdo) make migration harder since your content is locked into their platform.

### Do AI builders work for ecommerce?
Yes — Wix, Hostinger, and Bookmark all have built-in ecommerce features. For serious stores, we'd recommend Wix Business plan or using 10Web with WooCommerce.
