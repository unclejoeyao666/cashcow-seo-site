The best part? You don't need to code. We tested the leading no-code AI chatbot platforms to find which ones actually deliver.

- **24/7 availability** — capture leads at 3 AM without hiring night staff

## Implementation Tips

1. **Start with your FAQ** — train the bot on your 20 most common questions first
2. **Set up human handoff** — never leave a customer stuck in a bot loop
3. **Track resolution rate** — aim for 50%+ AI resolution before scaling
4. **A/B test greetings** — the opening message impacts engagement by 40%+
5. **Review conversations weekly** — find gaps in the bot's knowledge and retrain
