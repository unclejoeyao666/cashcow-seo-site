## AI Social Media Strategy Tips

1. **Don't auto-post everything.** Use AI to generate drafts, then add your personal touch before publishing.
2. **Repurpose aggressively.** One blog post should become 10+ social posts across platforms.
3. **Track what works.** Use analytics to double down on formats and topics your audience engages with.
4. **Platform-specific formatting.** What works on LinkedIn doesn't work on TikTok. Use tools that format for each platform.
5. **Engage authentically.** AI can help you respond faster, but genuine interaction builds real community.

## The Bottom Line

AI social media tools save 10-20 hours per week for most content creators. Start with Buffer's free tier to test the waters, scale to Hootsuite if you're managing a team, or go deep on a single platform with Taplio (LinkedIn) or Predis (visual content).

The goal isn't to replace your voice — it's to amplify it across more channels with less effort.
