## 4. Stable Diffusion (ComfyUI / Automatic1111) — Best for Control

**Price:** Free (self-hosted) | $10-25/month (cloud providers like RunPod)

The open-source choice. Stable Diffusion via ComfyUI gives you absolute control over every parameter — but requires technical knowledge and either your own GPU or cloud compute credits.

### Strengths
- **Complete control** — ControlNet, IP-Adapter, LoRA fine-tuning
- Free to run on your own hardware (RTX 3090+ recommended)
- Huge model ecosystem (Civitai, HuggingFace)
- Can fine-tune on your own style/characters (5-20 images)
- No content restrictions (use responsibly)

### Weaknesses
- Steep learning curve (ComfyUI workflow nodes)
- Setup time: 2-4 hours minimum to get running
- Need decent GPU (8GB VRAM minimum)
- Quality out-of-box inferior to Midjourney (requires tuning)

### Best For
- Developers building image generation features
- Creators with specific style needs (fine-tuning)
- High-volume generation (cost matters)
- Privacy-sensitive use cases (local processing)

### Pricing Verdict
Free on your own hardware = unbeatable for volume. Cloud providers (RunPod, Vast.ai) charge ~$0.20-0.50/hour for capable GPUs.

## Frequently Asked Questions

**Q: Can I use AI-generated images commercially?**  
A: Depends on the platform. Adobe Firefly: fully safe. Midjourney Pro+: yes. DALL-E 3: yes (per OpenAI terms). Stable Diffusion: depends on model license. Always check current ToS.

**Q: Which AI image generator produces the most realistic photos?**  
A: Midjourney v7 and Stable Diffusion with realistic checkpoints (like Realistic Vision or DreamShaper) produce the most photorealistic results in 2026.

**Q: Is AI image generation replacing stock photography?**  
A: For generic "business people shaking hands" type stock: yes, significantly. For authentic documentary photography: no. Adobe Stock reports AI-generated images now make up 15% of downloads.

**Q: How much does it cost to generate 1,000 AI images?**  
A: Midjourney Standard: ~$30-60. DALL-E 3 API: ~$4-8. Stable Diffusion cloud: ~$10-20. Local SD: electricity cost only.
