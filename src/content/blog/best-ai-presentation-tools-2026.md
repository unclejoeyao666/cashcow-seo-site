## Tips for Better AI Presentations

1. **Start with an outline**, not a blank prompt. The more structure you give, the better the output.
2. **Edit after generation.** AI gets you 80% there — your expertise adds the final 20%.
3. **Use your own images** for key slides. Stock photos are fine for filler, but real photos build trust.
4. **Keep it short.** AI makes it easy to over-generate. Ruthlessly cut slides that don't earn their place.
