## Security and Privacy Considerations

Before choosing, check:

1. **Data residency** — Where are recordings stored? (EU users: look for GDPR compliance + EU storage)
2. **Participant consent** — Does the tool notify participants they're being recorded?
3. **Data deletion** — Can you permanently delete recordings and transcripts?
4. **SOC 2 compliance** — Enterprise-grade security certification

**Best for privacy:** Tactiq (browser extension, less data sent to servers) and Fathom (clear privacy policy)

**Enterprise-grade:** Fireflies Business and tl;dv Business both offer SOC 2 Type II and GDPR compliance.
