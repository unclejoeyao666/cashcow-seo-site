## Performance & Speed Considerations

AI plugins that make API calls in real-time can slow your site. Here's how to minimize impact:

1. **Cache AI responses** — Most good plugins do this automatically
2. **Load chatbots asynchronously** — Shouldn't block page render
3. **Use a CDN** — Cloudflare caches static assets
4. **Disable unused features** — Only enable what you need

Run a PageSpeed test before and after installing any AI plugin to measure the impact.

## Privacy & GDPR

If you operate in the EU, ensure your chosen plugin:
- Processes data within the EU (or has a DPA)
- Has a cookie consent integration
- Allows users to delete their data
- Doesn't store conversations without consent

**Tidio and AI Engine** both have EU-compliant data processing agreements available.
