## 1. Roam Around

Roam Around generates full multi-day itineraries in seconds. Type a destination, travel dates, and interests — it returns a structured plan with attractions, restaurants, and travel times between stops.

**Pros:**
- Instant itinerary generation (under 10 seconds)
- Covers 200+ countries with local tips
- Export to Google Maps or PDF

**Cons:**
- Free tier limited to 3 trips/month
- Hotel/flight booking links are generic (not always cheapest)

**Pricing:** Free for basic use; Pro at $9.99/month unlocks unlimited trips and priority suggestions.

## 2. Layla AI

Layla works like a travel-savvy chatbot. Ask it anything — "3 days in Lisbon on a $500 budget" — and it builds a plan conversationally. You can refine with follow-ups: "swap the seafood restaurant for vegetarian options."

**Pros:**
- Natural conversation interface
- Real-time price awareness for flights and hotels
- Remembers preferences across sessions

**Cons:**
- Mobile app still in beta (web version is more stable)
- Limited offline access

**Pricing:** Completely free during 2026 growth phase.

## 3. Wonderplan

Wonderplan focuses on budget optimization. It asks for your total budget upfront, then allocates across flights, accommodation, food, and activities — showing exactly where your money goes.

**Pros:**
- Visual budget breakdown per day
- Suggests money-saving alternatives automatically
- Supports multi-city trips with cost comparison

**Cons:**
- Itineraries can feel generic for off-the-beaten-path destinations
- Slower generation (15-20 seconds)

**Pricing:** Free tier with ads; $7/month for ad-free and advanced budgeting.

👉 [Try Wonderplan](YOUR_AFFILIATE_LINK_WONDERPLAN)

## 4. iPlan.ai

iPlan.ai shines for group trips. Share a link with travel companions, everyone adds preferences, and the AI merges them into a consensus itinerary. Voting on activities is built in.

**Pros:**
- Collaborative planning with real-time sync
- Voting system resolves group disagreements
- Integrated expense splitting

**Cons:**
- Group features require all members to create accounts
- Free tier limited to 4 collaborators

**Pricing:** Free for solo; $5.99/month for groups up to 10.

👉 [Try iPlan.ai](YOUR_AFFILIATE_LINK_IPLAN)

## 5. Tripnotes

Tripnotes is built for research-heavy travelers who want deep local knowledge. It pulls from travel blogs, forums, and local guides to generate itineraries with cultural context and insider tips.

**Pros:**
- Rich cultural and historical context in suggestions
- Sources cited for every recommendation
- Offline PDF export with maps

**Cons:**
- Slower than competitors (relies on deeper research)
- UI feels cluttered on mobile

**Pricing:** Free basic; $10/month for premium with unlimited research depth.
