Upload a photo of any room and these tools generate redesigned versions in seconds. Here are the five best options in 2026.

## 1. Interior AI

Interior AI is the speed champion. Upload a room photo, pick a style (modern, Scandinavian, industrial, etc.), and get a redesigned image in under 15 seconds. The AI preserves room structure while swapping out furniture, paint, and decor.

**Pros:**
- 15+ design styles to choose from
- Preserves room dimensions and lighting
- High-resolution output suitable for mood boards

**Cons:**
- Free tier limited to 1 render
- Can struggle with unusual room layouts

**Pricing:** Free trial render; Pro at $29.99/month for unlimited.

## 2. RoomGPT

RoomGPT is the most accessible option. Completely free for basic use, it generates clean redesigns that focus on practical changes — furniture placement, color palettes, and lighting adjustments.

**Pros:**
- Generous free tier (5 renders/day)
- Clean, realistic outputs
- Simple interface — no learning curve

**Cons:**
- Fewer style options than competitors
- Output resolution capped at 1024px on free tier

**Pricing:** Free with limits; $9.99/month for HD output and priority rendering.

## 3. Collov AI

Collov specializes in photorealistic virtual staging — ideal for homeowners selling property. It generates images that look like professional photography, not AI renders.

**Pros:**

- Furniture suggestions link to real products
- Batch processing for multiple rooms

**Cons:**
- Higher price point
- Learning curve for custom style settings

**Pricing:** 3 free renders; $19/month for 50 renders.
