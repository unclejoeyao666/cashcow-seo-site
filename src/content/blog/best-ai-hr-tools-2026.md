# Best AI HR Tools 2026

AI is transforming HR — from resume screening to payroll to engagement. Here are the top 5 platforms delivering measurable hiring and retention ROI.

## 1. **Greenhouse with AI Screening**
- **Best for:** High-volume recruiting, tech companies
- **Core feature:** AI resume screening, interview scheduling automation
- **Use case:** 10x faster first-pass screening, reduce hiring time by 30%+
- **Pricing:** $1K–$5K+/month
- **Integrations:** Workday, Lever, LinkedIn
- **Why it wins:** Best-in-class compliance, audit trail for hiring decisions

## 2. **Rippling** — AI-Powered People Operations
- **Best for:** 50–5000 person companies, fully remote teams
- **Core feature:** Unified employee data, AI benefits recommendations, payroll
- **Use case:** Onboarding automation, benefits enrollment, time tracking
- **Pricing:** $8–$30/person/month
- **Integrations:** Slack, Google Workspace, 200+ apps
- **Why it wins:** Single source of truth replaces 5+ point solutions

## 3. **Workday** — Enterprise AI HR Suite
- **Best for:** Fortune 500, multinational corporations
- **Core feature:** Predictive analytics, succession planning, skills matching
- **Use case:** Identify flight risks, recommend internal mobility
- **Pricing:** Enterprise custom
- **Integrations:** SAP SuccessFactors, Cornerstone, ADP
- **Why it wins:** AI-driven insights reduce turnover by 15–25%

## 4. **Finch** — AI-Powered Recruiting Assistant
- **Best for:** Startups, fast-growing companies, DIY recruiters
- **Core feature:** Candidate sourcing, phone screen automation, video interview analysis
- **Use case:** Screen 1000s of candidates without recruiter involvement
- **Pricing:** $299–$999/month
- **Integrations:** LinkedIn, Greenhouse, Lever
- **Why it wins:** Cuts recruiting cost per hire by 40%+

## 5. **Guidepoint** — AI Employee Insights & Engagement
- **Best for:** Mid-market companies focused on employee retention
- **Core feature:** Pulse surveys, sentiment analysis, engagement scoring
- **Use case:** Detect disengagement early, improve retention
- **Pricing:** $5K–$20K/year
- **Integrations:** Slack, Microsoft Teams, Workplace
- **Why it wins:** Catch at-risk employees before they leave
