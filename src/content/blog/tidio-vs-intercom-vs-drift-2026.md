## Tidio — The SMB Champion

### What Is Tidio?

Tidio is a live chat and AI chatbot platform built for small and medium businesses — especially e-commerce stores. Their AI agent, **Lyro**, can resolve up to 70% of common support queries without human intervention. In 2026, Tidio has doubled down on making AI accessible to businesses that don't have a six-figure software budget.

### Tidio Pros ✅

- **Generous free plan** — 50 live chat conversations/month plus basic chatbot flows at zero cost
- **Lyro AI is shockingly good** — trains on your FAQ/knowledge base in minutes, handles multi-turn conversations naturally
- **Dead-simple setup** — you can be live in under 10 minutes, no developer needed
- **E-commerce integrations** — native Shopify, WooCommerce, and BigCommerce plugins with cart/order data access
- **Visual chatbot builder** — drag-and-drop flows anyone on your team can edit
- **Affordable scaling** — even the top-tier plan stays under $400/month

### Tidio Cons ❌

- **AI resolution limits on lower tiers** — Lyro conversations are capped; overages cost extra
- **Less suited for complex B2B** — if you need ABM, routing to AEs, or Salesforce-native workflows, you'll feel the gap
- **Analytics are basic** — reporting improved in 2026 but still doesn't match Intercom's depth
- **Limited automation beyond chat** — no built-in email sequences or product tours

### Who Should Use Tidio?

If you're a Shopify store doing $10K–$500K/month, a small SaaS with a lean support team, or any SMB that wants AI chat without enterprise pricing — Tidio is your best bet. Period.

## Intercom — The Full-Stack Customer Platform

### What Is Intercom?

Intercom has evolved from a simple messenger widget into a complete AI-first customer service platform. Their **Fin AI Agent** (powered by GPT-4 and custom models) can genuinely resolve support tickets end-to-end — not just deflect them. In 2026, Intercom is the platform mid-market SaaS companies measure everyone else against.

### Intercom Pros ✅

- **Fin AI is best-in-class** — consistently highest resolution rates in independent benchmarks
- **Unified inbox** — chat, email, social, SMS, WhatsApp all in one workspace
- **Proactive messaging** — trigger messages based on user behavior, page views, or events
- **Rich product tours & onboarding** — guide users through your app with interactive tooltips
- **Powerful reporting** — custom dashboards, AI performance metrics, CSAT tracking
- **Massive integration ecosystem** — 300+ apps including Salesforce, Stripe, Jira, and more

### Intercom Cons ❌

- **Price adds up fast** — per-seat pricing + per-resolution AI fees can surprise growing teams
- **Complexity** — the platform is powerful but the learning curve is real; expect 2–4 weeks to fully configure
- **No free plan** — 14-day trial only, then you're paying from day one
- **Fin AI costs extra** — $0.99 per resolution on top of your seat costs (though this often beats hiring agents)
- **Overkill for simple needs** — if you just need a chat widget, you're paying for a lot you won't use

### Who Should Use Intercom?

SaaS companies with 10–500 employees, teams handling 500+ support conversations/month, or anyone who wants a truly AI-first support platform and is willing to invest in it.

## Drift AI — The B2B Revenue Engine

### What Is Drift AI?

Drift pioneered conversational marketing and was acquired by **Salesloft** in late 2023. In 2026, Drift AI has been deeply integrated into Salesloft's revenue orchestration platform, making it the go-to for B2B sales teams that want AI chatbots focused on pipeline generation, not just support ticket deflection.

### Drift AI Pros ✅

- **Built for revenue** — every feature is designed to book meetings, qualify leads, and accelerate pipeline
- **Account-based targeting** — show different chatbot experiences to target accounts vs. anonymous visitors
- **Salesforce-native** — bi-directional sync, lead routing based on CRM data, activity logging
- **AI-powered meeting booking** — the bot checks rep calendars and books qualified meetings automatically
- **Conversation intelligence** — records, transcribes, and analyzes sales conversations
- **Enterprise-grade security** — SOC 2 Type II, GDPR, SSO, and role-based access

### Drift AI Cons ❌

- **Expensive** — pricing starts around $2,500/month and scales up quickly
- **Not for support** — if your primary use case is customer support, you're buying the wrong tool
- **Complex implementation** — expect a 4–8 week onboarding process with their team
- **Salesloft lock-in** — increasingly bundled with Salesloft's broader platform
- **Overkill for SMBs** — if you have fewer than 5 sales reps, the ROI math probably doesn't work

### Who Should Use Drift AI?

B2B companies with dedicated sales teams, $1M+ ARR, and a focus on inbound pipeline. If you're already in the Salesloft ecosystem, it's a no-brainer. For everyone else, think hard about whether you need this level of firepower.

## Head-to-Head: Real-World Scenarios

### Scenario 1: E-commerce Store (50K monthly visitors)

**Winner: Tidio** 🏆

A Shopify store processing 200 orders/day needs a chatbot that handles "Where is my order?", "Can I return this?", and "Do you have this in size L?" automatically. Tidio's Lyro AI trains on your product catalog and handles this beautifully for $59–$100/month. Intercom would cost 3–5x more. Drift isn't even built for this.

### Scenario 2: SaaS Company (20-person support team)

**Winner: Intercom** 🏆

When you have 20 support agents handling thousands of tickets across chat, email, and social — and you need AI to resolve 80% of L1 queries — Intercom's Fin AI is unmatched. Yes, you'll pay $2,000–$4,000/month, but the ROI from reduced headcount needs often pays for itself in months.

### Scenario 3: B2B Enterprise (pipeline-driven)

**Winner: Drift AI** 🏆

Your website gets 100K monthly visitors. You need to identify which ones are from target accounts, engage them with personalized chatbot experiences, qualify them, and book meetings with the right AE — all automatically. That's literally what Drift was built for.

## Integration Ecosystem

All three platforms integrate with major tools, but the depth varies:

- **Tidio** shines with e-commerce platforms (Shopify, WooCommerce, BigCommerce) and simple CRM connections via Zapier. It also connects natively to Instagram, Facebook Messenger, and WhatsApp.

- **Intercom** has the broadest ecosystem with 300+ native integrations. Salesforce, HubSpot, Stripe, Jira, GitHub, Slack — if your team uses it, Intercom probably integrates with it.

- **Drift AI** goes deep rather than wide. Its Salesforce integration is arguably the best in the category. 6sense, Marketo, and Salesloft integrations are enterprise-grade. But if you're not in the Salesforce ecosystem, options narrow quickly.
