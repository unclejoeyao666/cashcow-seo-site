## What AI Marketing Tools Can't Do (Yet)

Be realistic about limitations:

1. **Strategy** — AI doesn't know your business, customers, or competitive position. You still need to set direction.
2. **Relationship building** — Real partnerships, influencer relationships, and community require human judgment.
3. **Crisis management** — Never let AI handle PR crises or sensitive customer situations.
4. **Original research** — AI can synthesize existing information but can't conduct real market research.

## FAQ

**What's the best free AI marketing tool in 2026?**
Claude's free tier is surprisingly capable for basic copywriting. Buffer's free plan handles social scheduling. For SEO, Google Search Console + a free NeuronWriter trial can get you started.

**Can AI replace my marketing team?**
No — but it can make a small team perform like a large one. AI handles execution; humans handle strategy, relationships, and judgment.

**Which AI tool is best for B2B marketing?**
Claude for content, ActiveCampaign for nurture sequences, and LinkedIn + Buffer for social. For lead gen, consider AI-powered tools like Clay or Apollo.

**Is AI-generated content penalized by Google?**
Google's official position is that helpful, accurate content ranks regardless of how it was created. The key is quality and relevance — AI tools used by skilled marketers produce content that ranks.

**How do I maintain brand voice with AI tools?**
Create a "brand voice prompt" — a paragraph describing your tone, audience, and key differentiators. Paste it at the start of every Claude conversation. Jasper's brand voice feature automates this for teams.
