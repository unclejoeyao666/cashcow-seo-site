## The 3-Tier System

HubSpot's affiliate program runs three tiers based on monthly sign-up volume:

### 🥉 Starter (0–29 sign-ups/month)
- **30% recurring commission** on sales (up to 1 year)
- Access to affiliate resource center
- Performance optimization opportunities

### 🥈 Sprocket (30–99 sign-ups/month)
- **30% recurring + cost per sign-up bonus**
- Enhanced performance reporting
- Regular check-ins with dedicated affiliate manager
- Co-branded landing pages

### 🥇 Elite (100+ sign-ups/month)
- **Custom commission structure**
- Bespoke website audit
- Co-branded promotional assets
- White-glove support
