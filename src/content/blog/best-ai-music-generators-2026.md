## 1. Suno — Best Overall AI Music Generator

**Pricing:** Free tier (10 songs/day) · Pro from $10/mo · Premier $30/mo

Suno remains the category leader in 2026. Type a prompt like "upbeat lo-fi hip hop, rainy day vibes" and get a full song — vocals included — in under 60 seconds.

**Pros:**
- Vocal generation with lyrics (auto or custom)
- Multiple genres and styles
- Commercial license on paid plans
- V4 model produces near-radio-quality output

**Cons:**
- Free tier songs are non-commercial
- Occasional vocal artifacts on complex harmonies

**Best for:** YouTubers, podcasters, indie game developers

## 2. Udio — Best for Realistic Vocals

**Pricing:** Free tier · Standard $10/mo · Pro $30/mo

Udio's strength is vocal realism. Its latest model handles multi-language lyrics, emotional range, and genre-switching better than any competitor.

**Pros:**
- Best-in-class vocal quality
- Supports 10+ languages
- Audio inpainting and remix tools
- Stems export for editing

**Cons:**
- Slightly slower generation than Suno
- UI can be overwhelming for beginners

**Best for:** Musicians, content creators who need vocals

[Try Udio →](https://udio.com/?ref=cashcow)

## 3. AIVA — Best for Cinematic & Classical Scores

**Pricing:** Free · Standard €11/mo · Pro €33/mo

AIVA specializes in orchestral, cinematic, and classical compositions. If you need a film score or epic trailer music, AIVA is unmatched.

**Pros:**
- MIDI + audio export
- Fine-grained control over instruments and structure
- Copyright-free on paid plans
- Great for film/game scoring

**Cons:**
- Not ideal for pop/electronic genres
- No vocal generation
- Steeper learning curve

**Best for:** Filmmakers, game studios, composers

[Try AIVA →](https://aiva.ai/?ref=cashcow)

## 4. Soundraw — Best for Quick Background Music

**Pricing:** Free trial · Creator $16.99/mo · Artist $29.99/mo

Soundraw takes a different approach: instead of full AI generation, it lets you customize pre-generated loops by adjusting energy, tempo, and instruments in real time.

**Pros:**
- Instant customization without prompting
- Consistent quality for background use
- YouTube & social media safe
- Simple, clean interface

**Cons:**
- Less creative variety than Suno/Udio
- No vocal tracks
- Higher starting price

**Best for:** Social media managers, video editors, ad agencies

[Try Soundraw →](https://soundraw.io/?ref=cashcow)
