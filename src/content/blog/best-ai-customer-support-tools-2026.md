## How to Choose the Right AI Support Tool

### Decision Framework

**Pick Zendesk if:** You handle 500+ tickets/day, need enterprise-grade analytics, and have budget for per-agent pricing.

**Pick Intercom if:** You're a SaaS company wanting autonomous AI resolution (Fin) and strong in-app messaging.

**Pick Freshdesk if:** You want the best price-to-performance ratio and your team has 5-50 agents.

**Pick Tidio if:** You run an e-commerce store and want quick-setup chat + AI with shopping-aware automation.

**Pick HubSpot if:** Your team is already in the HubSpot ecosystem and wants unified CRM + support data.

### Key Factors to Evaluate

1. **Ticket Volume:** High volume (500+/day) → Zendesk or Intercom. Low volume → Freshdesk or Tidio.
2. **AI Autonomy:** Want AI to fully resolve tickets? → Intercom Fin or Tidio Lyro. Want AI-assisted agents? → Zendesk or Freshdesk Freddy.
3. **Integration Needs:** Deep e-commerce → Tidio. CRM-centric → HubSpot. API-first → Intercom.
4. **Budget:** Under $500/mo → Freshdesk or Tidio. $500-2000/mo → Intercom. $2000+/mo → Zendesk Enterprise.

## Frequently Asked Questions

### Which AI support tool has the best free tier?
Freshdesk offers the most generous free plan — up to 10 agents with basic ticketing and knowledge base. Tidio's free plan is limited to 50 conversations/month but includes live chat.

### Can AI support tools fully replace human agents?
Not yet. The best tools (Intercom Fin, Tidio Lyro) can autonomously resolve 40-60% of routine inquiries, but complex issues, emotional situations, and escalations still need human agents.

### How long does implementation typically take?
Tidio: 1-2 hours. Freshdesk/HubSpot: 1-2 days. Intercom: 3-5 days. Zendesk Enterprise: 2-4 weeks.

### What ROI should I expect from AI customer support?
Most companies see 15-30% reduction in support costs within 3 months, primarily through faster resolution times and reduced ticket volume via self-service.
