## FAQs

### What's the single most impactful AI tool for ecommerce?
Email marketing (Klaviyo). The ROI is proven, the free plan is generous, and automated flows (cart abandonment, welcome series) generate revenue 24/7 with minimal setup.

### Do AI tools work with Shopify, WooCommerce, and BigCommerce?
All tools in this list integrate with major ecommerce platforms. Most offer one-click Shopify app installs. WooCommerce and BigCommerce typically require a plugin or API setup.

### How much should an ecommerce store spend on AI tools?
A good rule of thumb: 1-3% of revenue. A store doing $50K/month can justify $500-1,500/month in AI tools if they deliver measurable ROI. Start free, measure, and scale.

### Will AI replace ecommerce managers?
No — but ecommerce managers who use AI will replace those who don't. AI handles repetitive tasks (writing descriptions, sending emails, adjusting prices) so you can focus on strategy, brand, and customer relationships.
