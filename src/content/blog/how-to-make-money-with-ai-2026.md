## Common Mistakes to Avoid

1. **Trying everything at once** — Pick one method, go deep, get results, then diversify
2. **Undercharging** — AI reduces your costs, not your value. Charge for outcomes.
3. **Ignoring quality** — AI generates volume. You add judgment. Don't skip the editing step.
4. **Waiting to be "ready"** — Ship imperfect. Iterate. The market will tell you what to fix.
5. **Building in secret** — Document publicly. Your learning journey is valuable content.
