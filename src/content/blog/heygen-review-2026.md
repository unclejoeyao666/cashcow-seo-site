## Key Features Tested

### 1. AI Avatar Quality — 9/10

HeyGen's stock avatar library has 100+ professional-looking avatars across ethnicities, ages, and presentation styles. The lip-sync is eerily accurate — even skeptical colleagues couldn't immediately tell these weren't real people.

**Custom avatars** are where HeyGen really shines. Record a 2-minute consent video, and within 24-48 hours you have a digital twin that can narrate any script you feed it. I tested this for client explainer videos — the quality was production-ready at 80% of cases, needed minor adjustments on 20%.

### 2. Video Translation — 9.5/10

This is HeyGen's killer feature. Upload a video in English, and it outputs a version in Spanish, German, Japanese, or 37 other languages — with the speaker's lips resynced to the new audio. 

Real test: Translated a 3-minute product demo from English to German. Turnaround: 8 minutes. Quality: Passed internal review without changes. The alternative (human dubbing + resync): ~$500 and 3 business days.

**Translation ROI is immediate for anyone with international customers.**

### 3. Text-to-Video — 8/10

Type your script, select avatar, choose background, add your brand assets → video renders in 3-7 minutes. For training content, FAQ videos, product updates, or social media content, this workflow is genuinely fast.

Limitations: Maximum video length varies by plan (5 minutes on Creator, 30 minutes on Business). Complex scenes with multiple speakers require workarounds.

### 4. Template Library — 7.5/10

50+ templates for common formats (LinkedIn post, explainer, product demo, training module). Quality is decent. I found myself customizing heavily anyway, so templates are more of a starting point than a final output.

### 5. API & Integrations — 8/10

HeyGen has a solid REST API, Zapier integration, and direct connections to HubSpot and Salesforce. For automated video personalization at scale (e.g., "Hello [FirstName], here's your onboarding video"), the API is the key enabler. Documentation is clear, implementation took ~2 hours.

**Is HeyGen's AI avatar realistic enough for professional use?**  
Yes, for most professional contexts — marketing videos, training content, product explainers, LinkedIn videos. For executive communications or high-stakes presentations, some stakeholders may still prefer human-filmed video.

**How long does it take to clone your own avatar?**  
You record a 2-minute consent/training video, then HeyGen processes it in 24-48 hours. Once ready, generating a new video with your avatar takes 3-7 minutes.

**Does HeyGen's translation maintain the original speaker's voice?**  
With the Voice Clone + Translation feature (Business plan), yes — it creates a language-specific clone of your voice. Standard translation uses AI-generated voice that matches the original's tone and cadence but isn't a perfect voice match.

**Is there a free trial?**  
Yes, you get 1 free credit (1 minute of video) to test the product without a credit card. Business plan has a 7-day trial.

**How does HeyGen handle content rights for custom avatars?**  
You must record and consent to your own likeness being used. HeyGen has strict policies against creating avatars of other people without consent, and violations result in account termination.

**Can HeyGen integrate with my CRM for personalized video at scale?**  
Yes, via API or native Zapier/HubSpot/Salesforce integrations. This is one of the most powerful use cases — auto-generate personalized "Hi [Name]" outreach videos.
