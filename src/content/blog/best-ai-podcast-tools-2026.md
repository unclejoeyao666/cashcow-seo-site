## 1. Descript — Best All-in-One Podcast Editor

Descript remains the gold standard for AI-powered podcast editing in 2026. Its text-based editing approach lets you edit audio by editing the transcript — delete a word from the text, and it's removed from the audio.

**Key AI Features:**
- **Filler word removal** — automatically detects and removes "um," "uh," and other filler words
- **Studio Sound** — enhances audio quality to sound like a professional studio recording
- **Voice cloning (Overdub)** — fix mistakes by typing what you meant to say in your own voice
- **AI show notes & summaries** — generates episode descriptions automatically

**Pricing:** Free tier available, Pro starts at $24/month.

**Best for:** Podcasters who want a single tool for recording, editing, transcription, and publishing.

## How to Choose the Right AI Podcast Tool

Consider these factors:

1. **Your workflow stage** — Do you need recording, editing, or distribution help?
2. **Solo vs. interview format** — Remote recording quality matters more for interviews
3. **Content repurposing goals** — If social clips matter, Opus Clip excels
4. **Budget** — Free tiers exist for all five tools
5. **Technical comfort** — Podcastle is simplest; Descript offers the most control
