## Pro Tips for AI Photo Editing

1. **Start with the best source image possible** — AI enhances, but can't create detail from nothing
2. **Use batch processing** for product catalogs — saves hours
3. **Check edges carefully** on background removal — AI occasionally misses fine details
4. **Combine tools** — Remove.bg for cutouts → Canva for final design
5. **Save originals** — always keep unedited copies
