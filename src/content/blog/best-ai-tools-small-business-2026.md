## How to Choose the Right AI Tools for Your Business

### Step 1: Identify Your Biggest Time Drains
List the 5 tasks that consume the most time in your business. These are your highest-ROI automation targets.

### Step 2: Calculate the Cost of Not Automating
- Current time spent × hourly rate = annual cost of manual work
- Compare to AI tool subscription cost
- Look for >5× ROI before committing

### Step 3: Start with One Tool
Tool overload is real. Pick the one tool that addresses your #1 pain point. Master it before adding more.

### Step 4: Measure and Iterate
Track specific metrics before and after implementing each tool:
- Hours saved per week
- Output volume (content pieces, leads, responses)
- Revenue impact
