# Best AI Legal Tools 2026

Legal professionals are adopting AI to accelerate contract review, due diligence, and document generation. Here are the top 5 AI legal tools delivering measurable ROI.

## 1. **Harvey AI** — Enterprise Contract Intelligence
- **Best for:** In-house legal teams, large law firms
- **Core feature:** Retrieval-augmented generation (RAG) for contract analysis
- **Use case:** Flag risks in M&A docs, identify non-standard clauses
- **Pricing:** Enterprise (custom)
- **Integrations:** Salesforce, NetDocuments
- **Why it wins:** Trained on real legal corpus, reduces review time by 40%+

## 2. **Clause** — Playbook-Driven Contract Automation
- **Best for:** Contract-heavy operations (vendor, NDA, employment)
- **Core feature:** Reusable playbooks + clause library
- **Use case:** Generate compliant contract templates in minutes
- **Pricing:** $200–$500/month (team tiers)
- **Integrations:** Slack, Salesforce, Microsoft Teams
- **Why it wins:** Pre-built templates for 50+ common contract types

## 3. **Everlaw Relate** — Legal Document Search & Analytics
- **Best for:** Law firms, corporate legal departments
- **Core feature:** AI-powered search across unstructured legal documents
- **Use case:** Find relevant precedent clauses, identify pattern risks
- **Pricing:** Per-seat + data volume
- **Integrations:** Counsel Link, Logikcull
- **Why it wins:** 10x faster document review in litigation

## 4. **LawGeex** — AI Contract Review at Scale
- **Best for:** Contract procurement, vendor management
- **Core feature:** Automated risk flagging, policy compliance checks
- **Use case:** Pre-screen vendor contracts before legal review
- **Pricing:** $5K–$20K+/year
- **Integrations:** Slack, Salesforce, Ironclad
- **Why it wins:** Catches 95%+ of known risk patterns

## 5. **Spellbook** — AI Drafting Assistant for Lawyers
- **Best for:** Individual practitioners, small law firms
- **Core feature:** GPT-4 powered document drafting in Word
- **Use case:** Draft memos, motions, discovery responses
- **Pricing:** $35/month (individual)
- **Integrations:** Microsoft Word, OpenAI
- **Why it wins:** Context-aware, trains on your own document library
