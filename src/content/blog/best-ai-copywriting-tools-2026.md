## The Honest Problem With AI Copywriting in 2026

In 2024, you could publish AI content and get SEO traffic. In 2026? Google's HCU updates have made that nearly impossible unless your content demonstrates **actual expertise and specificity**.

The tools that survive are the ones that help you add human value faster—not replace your voice entirely.

Here's what separates the winners from the garbage:

1. **Memory and context retention** — Can it remember your brand voice across sessions?
2. **Workflow automation** — Does it connect to your CMS, email platform, or ad manager?
3. **Factual accuracy** — Does it hallucinate statistics that will embarrass your brand?

## How to Choose: The $100 Rule

Before subscribing to any AI copy tool, ask:

> *"If this tool saves me 2 hours/month and my time is worth $50/hour, does it pay for itself?"*

For most solopreneurs:
- **$20-40/month tools**: Need to save you ~30-60 minutes/month to break even
- **$100+/month tools**: Require regular use with measurable conversion lift

Use the free trials aggressively. Most tools offer 7-14 day trials—run a real campaign with each one.
